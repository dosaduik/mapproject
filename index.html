Oliver Halloween Map Game

It's Halloween and something just isn't right in Oliver
There seem to be monsters everywhere! Can you clear the whole neighboorhood of monsters?

You will need to walk around the neighboorhood with the game open to navigate the map.
Your avatar in the game is Jack the anthropomorphic jack-o-lantern!

Once you reach a haunted area (scary face on the map), you will hear a scary sound and then you will have to fight off attacking monsters! More and more monsters will try to stop you!

Fight the monsters by swiping over them with you finger until they disappear.

Don't worry, if you die you can just start again....forever!!!!
Good Luck!!
